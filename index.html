rules

IX 2020 is a Designathon comprising of 2 rounds (Initial and Final)

A team can consist of a minimum of two members and a maximum of three

A team should comprise of students from the same university. Combining with another university will result in disqualification.

On completion of a prototype design at the end of a round, the leader of each team is required to fill in a google form with the prototype link and documentation
