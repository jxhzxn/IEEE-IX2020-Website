About

Conducted by the IEEE Student Branch of
Informatics Institute of Technology 2019/2020,
IX 2020 is the first ever virtual inter-university
designathon in Sri Lanka encompassed with
a workshop and competitions that put to test
the skills of aspiring UI/UX Designers.

Rules

IX 2020 is a Designathon comprising of 2 rounds (Initial and Final)

A team can consist of a minimum of two members and a maximum of three

Teams cannot be formed by combining students from other universities and doing so would result in disqualification
